Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate tech enthusiasts and researchers focused on sustainable computing. Initially hosted in a university lecture hall, our focus on ethical technology quickly resonated with the wider industry. Over the years, TechCon has grown into an international event, becoming the essential platform for launching groundbreaking ideas and fostering global tech partnerships.

We proudly celebrated our tenth anniversary in 2020, achieving record attendance and establishing our permanent mission to prioritize technology that benefits humanity and the planet.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to bridge the gap between innovation and responsibility. We aim to:

  • **Promote Sustainable Development:** Showcase technologies that minimize environmental impact.
  • **Foster Ethical AI:** Lead discussions on governance and bias in artificial intelligence.
  • **Empower the Next Generation:** Provide mentorship and networking opportunities for students and early-career professionals.

Our driving principle is that technology must serve human progress, not complicate it.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in the global tech landscape. Below are a few highlights from our past events:

Dr. Anya Sharma (AI Ethics Pioneer)

Dr. Sharma headlined TechCon 2021, presenting her groundbreaking framework for transparent machine learning models. Her work continues to set the standard for ethical compliance in software development.

Kenji Tanaka (Cloud Infrastructure Architect)

In 2018, Kenji Tanaka shared his vision for serverless architecture that drastically reduces energy consumption, inspiring a generation of sustainable cloud practices.
